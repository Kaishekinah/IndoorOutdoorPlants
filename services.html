Our Services

Plant Sales

The primary service of a plant store is, of course, selling plants. This includes a diverse selection of indoor and outdoor plants, ranging from small succulents to large trees.

Delivery services for large or delicate plants, as well as installation assistance for customers who may need help setting up their new greenery.

Online Sales and Shipping
